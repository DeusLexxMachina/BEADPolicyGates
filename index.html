The dependency chain problem
EO 14365 created a 4-step sequential lock: DOJ list (§3) → Commerce eval (§4) → NTIA policy notice (§5a) → FCC proceeding (§6). The §3 DOJ list — the legal predicate for everything downstream — was due ~Jan 10 and has never published. Every gate downstream is frozen at step one.
Lutnick's three deadlines
Three sequentially slipped commitments: "Shortly" (Feb testimony) → March 11 (staff-stated, missed) → "Two months" (Apr 22 testimony = late June). Each offered without acknowledging the prior miss. Q3 2026 remains the structurally grounded estimate absent direct Secretary intervention in Commerce GC clearance.
The $21B framing trap
"We've executed universal broadband for $21B" (Lutnick, Feb + Apr hearings) conflates grant agreement execution with broadband deployment. No fiber is in the ground. The $21B is a nondeployment savings pool — not surplus over a completed goal. This framing is load-bearing for the argument that the pool can fund "other things exceptional for America."
